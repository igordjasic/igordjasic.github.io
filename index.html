Welcome!

Photography has been a hobby of mine for a while now. I mostly take still, quiet landscape shots with harsh lightning but there is a problem with my pictures. Because I don't take many pictures of people, I don't show them to many people so I can't improve! Thats where you come in. This website will let you help me by allowing you to select one of my photos from a gallery and leave a professional critique of it, positive or negative. so that I can get better.

Sign Up

First head to the SIGN UP page, here you need to make an account before you access the image page. This section will ask for your first and last name, age, email, and student number. After you have filled in all the requirements, click the submit button and the images page will become available to you!

Images

After you open the images tab, you'll be given the choice between many pictures I have taken personally. Select a picture by clicking on it, when the picture is selected it will become grayed out. If you wish to pick another picture, click your first selection again before picking another one as you cannot have two images selection. After you have made your choice, open the Review tab.

Review

Once you've chosen the photo that you want to review, use the textbox available to you to write down your insightful commentary. Make sure to set the mood before you write with some background noise and or music to improve your focus. Once you are finished writing, click the submit button to display your review on the front page.
